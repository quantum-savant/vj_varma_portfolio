About Me

Hi, I'm Vijay Varma

I'm a freelance web designer and frontend developer based in Lucknow. I work with local businesses — shops, clinics, consultants, and service providers — who need a proper website but don't want the complexity of working with a large agency.

Most of my clients come to me because they need something that works well, looks professional, and doesn't require them to manage a lot of technical details. I handle the design, the code, and make sure the site loads quickly on mobile — because that's where most of your customers are browsing from.

I keep projects simple and direct. You'll deal with me personally from start to finish, and I'll make sure you understand what you're getting before we begin.

What I Do

Services

I offer three focused services for businesses that need a web presence built properly.

Landing Pages

A single-page site focused on one clear goal — getting visitors to call you, fill a form, or make a purchase. Works well for promotions, services, or new products.

Business Websites

A complete website with multiple pages — home, about, services, and contact. Designed to present your business clearly and help customers find what they need.

Website Redesign

If you have an existing website that looks dated or isn't working on mobile, I can rebuild it with a clean, modern design while keeping your content intact.
